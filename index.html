Meet Our Mascot!

Model Club is back with yet another invigorating event "Case-O-Heist", and we are equally thrilled to introduce our mascot "Sofia". Drawing inspiration from the theme, "Money Heist", Sofía is a ferret belonging to Marseille, a character in the web-series. Ferret is a curious animal and if caged, it should be taken out daily to exercise and satisfy it's curiosity. We call upon all the curious minds to probe into some of the intricate real life problems and come up with their newfangled solutions.
