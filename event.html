sobre el evento

The event is a National Level Competition to be organized by MODEL CLUB, BIT Sindri , aimed at highlighting the problems faced by the industries in different spheres.

Under Case-O-Heist we aim to bring attention to problems within production plants of companies, data and security of organizations, patient management challenges of hospitals, management of waste in industries and housing colonies and many more.

A 72-hours long virtual evaluation will be conducted to test the proposed solutions against standard judgement criterion.

Who can Participate!

In the year 2016, I Co-founded a startup, at the age of 20, which was a waste management company. We provided a free doorstep service to collect junk/scrap material from different locations and the environment-friendly service to create go green.

  • All engineering and non-engineering students are allowed to take part.
  • The startup was featured on the headlines of different newspapers and local news channels.Students will be participating in teams, with a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • There is no participation fee at any stage during the competition
